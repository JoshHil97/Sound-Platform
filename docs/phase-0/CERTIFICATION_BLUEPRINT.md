# Sound Academy Certification Blueprint

## Certification Principles

Certification is not awarded for watching lessons. It requires evidence that the volunteer can perform real church sound tasks safely, explain decisions, recover from common faults and serve with the right ministry posture.

Every certification requires:

- Required modules completed
- Knowledge assessments passed
- Practical assessments signed off
- Sound Lab diagnostic exercises completed
- Service observations logged
- Mentor feedback recorded
- Evidence reviewed
- Renewal date assigned

## Certification Levels

### Foundations Certified

Mission: safe beginner participation.

Required competencies:

- Explain ministry purpose and signal flow.
- Identify microphone, DI, stagebox, X32, bus, P16, Dante and Logic roles.
- Set safe gain on one speech source under supervision.
- Trace one FOH path and one livestream path.

Required evidence:

- Foundations quiz pass
- Signal path sketch
- Gain drill mentor observation
- One rehearsal shadowing note

Renewal: annual refresher on safety, gain and troubleshooting basics.

### FOH Operator Certified

Mission: supervised Sunday operation.

Required competencies:

- Load approved showfile.
- Patch, name and gain service inputs.
- Run line check.
- Mix speech and worship at FOH.
- Build monitor/P16 sends.
- Prepare wireless microphones.
- Handle common live faults.

Required evidence:

- Operator quizzes
- X32 patch-name-gain practical
- Wireless prep sign-off
- One full rehearsal mix observation
- Two live-service observations

Renewal: annual service observation and incident-review refresher.

### Livestream Operator Certified

Mission: run dedicated stream mix.

Required competencies:

- Verify Dante/DVS into Logic.
- Open and validate approved Logic template.
- Mix speech, vocal, worship, choir and audience paths.
- Use Waves/Logic processing safely.
- Monitor loudness and limiter behavior.
- Confirm OBS or encoder audio handoff.

Required evidence:

- Logic template walkthrough
- Dante-to-Logic screenshot evidence
- Sound Lab translation/clipping/limiting exercises
- Two observed livestream rehearsals
- One live stream operation under mentor review

Renewal: semiannual Logic template and loudness-policy review.

### Systems Engineer Certified

Mission: own routing, Dante, fault isolation and virtual soundcheck workflows.

Required competencies:

- Manage X32 routing, matrices, scenes and snippets safely.
- Configure Dante Controller subscriptions and DVS.
- Explain clock leader, sample rate and latency.
- Diagnose cross-domain faults.
- Document changes and rollback plans.

Required evidence:

- Dante route build/break/restore exercise
- X32 routing documentation
- Virtual soundcheck/review exercise
- Timed troubleshooting assessment
- Three live services observed or supervised

Renewal: annual Dante/network and routing audit.

### Senior Engineer Certified

Mission: coach and assess others.

Required competencies:

- Mentor trainees.
- Conduct practical assessments.
- Lead service debriefs.
- Maintain SOPs and incident review.
- Approve lower-level sign-offs.

Required evidence:

- Two observed mentor sessions
- Assessment rubric calibration
- SOP contribution
- Service quality review
- Technical Director approval

Renewal: annual mentor calibration.

### Technical Director Certified

Mission: govern production systems and certification.

Required competencies:

- Own architecture and redundancy.
- Approve major routing/template/network changes.
- Manage inventory lifecycle.
- Govern certifications and admin permissions.
- Lead platform content quality.

Required evidence:

- System architecture document
- Change-management case review
- Certification governance review
- Inventory/procurement plan
- Leadership approval

Renewal: annual architecture and governance review.

## Progression Rules

- A trainee cannot attempt independent service operation before Foundations completion.
- FOH Operator requires Foundations certification.
- Livestream Operator requires Foundations plus Dante/Logic prerequisites.
- Systems Engineer requires FOH or Livestream certification plus Dante/X32 advanced modules.
- Senior Engineer requires Systems Engineer and mentor calibration.
- Technical Director requires Senior Engineer or equivalent leadership approval.

## Mentor Requirements

Mentors must record:

- Observation date
- Service/rehearsal context
- Task observed
- What went well
- Risk or correction
- Pass/retry decision
- Evidence links
- Next assigned practice
